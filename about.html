What is $BROKEN?

$BROKEN is a meme token created to joke on the fact of being broke. In 2025 everyone is struggling in some way, given the fact that everything is crazy at the moment, from prices to job markets.

This token aims to build a community around it, made from those that are tired of struggles and at the same time, want to help other people, by contributing actively to the project, to achieve something.

The main objective of $BROKEN, is to give everyone involved the possibility to realize a dream, from buying a car to studying or to quit their shitty job.

The web and expecially crypto, is a jungle where everyone tries to scam or prevail, $BROKEN wants to bring unity and purpose.

Last but not least, the project aims to establish donations to charities and other associations, if not creating it's own charity to help building a better future.

Hopefully if the community around $BROKEN is successful, we could one day achieve our dreams, and maybe, help others to achieve theirs.
